We Build

Your CI/CD pipelines with Kubernetes!

We use powerful and flexible open-source frameworks that provide kubernetes-style resources for declaring CI/CD-style pipelines such as Tekton and ArgoCD, we make deploying faster and allow you to deploy more often.

We Design

Your infrastructure and deployment strategies

We provide production-grade design/architecture ADRs (Architecture Decision Record) for your clusters and/or identify gaps and issues for your existing ones. We can also determine all enhancements including deployment strategies such as blue/green, canary and define the costs and benefits of proposed solutions.

We Run

highly secure, scalable & reliable kubernetes clusters

We evangelize SRE best practices for operating kubernetes clusters, and automate everyting from infrastructure to day-to-day tasks using bash/goLang scripts and k8s operators. We also assist you with all aspects of operational security and compliance, and seek out potential threats using Falco, Kyverno, OPA/Gatekeeper, etc. We work mainly with Google Cloud Platform, Prometheus/Grafana for observability, EFK for logging and Litmus for Chaos Engineering!
